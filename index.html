The Poultry Hub

The Poultry Hub provides a platform for all things relating to poultry shows. Including a brilliant interface allowing secretaries to quickly setup a show. Exhibitors are also encouraged to use The Poultry Hub to send in entry forms. The Poultry Hub is the next generation of show secretary software, allowing clerks to enter data online and in real time! The data from clerks is instantly sent to the secretary where award specific data can be retrieved with a click, eliminating secretaries' need to scramble through all of the clerk sheets before the awards ceremony.

Poultry Manager offers show secretaries an effecient way of organizing club shows.

Exhibitors are displayed in a easily manipulated chart.

Exhibitors entries are editable on the fly.

All birds can be seen from the birds tab.

Digital clerk sheets are viewable from the clerk sheets tab.

Printable clerk sheets are also created through the clerk sheets tab.
